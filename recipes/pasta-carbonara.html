Pasta Carbonara Recipe

Description

A rich, savory dish perfect for lunch or a light dinner.

With a smooth, peppery sauce mixed with the bold flavor of bacon, pasta carbonara is a classic dish that works every time!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan Cheese block
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti
  2. Chop bacon into small chunks
  3. Lightly fry bacon in pan
  4. Make sauce:
    • Separate egg yolks into bowl
    • Shred parmesan cheese into bowl
    • Grind pepper into bowl
    • Stir ingredients until thoroughly mixed
  5. Drain pasta, keep cup of pasta water
  6. Combine pasta with bacon, remove heat
  7. Add sauce slowly, tossing pasta
  8. Top with parmesan, egregious pepper
  9. Bon appetit!
