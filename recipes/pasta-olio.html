Pasta Aglio e Olio Recipe

Description

Pasta aglio e olio is an Italian dish of spaghetti in a garlic oil sauce, topped with parsley and lemon.

The dish is fast and easy to make, and is a great, light, flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Parsley
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water
  2. Heat olive oil in pan
  3. Slice garlic
  4. Put sliced garlic into heated oil
  5. As garlic browns, chop parsley
  6. Drain pasta, saving cup of pasta water
  7. Add red pepper flakes to oil
  8. Transfer pasta to oil mixture, toss to coat it
  9. Add pasta water for more cohesive sauce
  10. Top with chopped parsley and lemon juice
  11. Bon appetit!
