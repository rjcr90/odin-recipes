Lemon Pepper Chicken

Description

Want a zestier version of a standard dish? Try lemon pepper chicken!

Add a new world of flavor to your dinner with a tart, citrusy take on chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Lemons
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh pepper
  • Garlic
  • Lemon Pepper

Steps

  1. Mince garlic
  2. Cut lemons in half, make some lemon slices
  3. Place chicken in bag, add all ingredients
  4. Squeeze lemon juice into bag
  5. Let chicken marinate anywhere from 8h to 2 days
  6. Grill chicken until edges begin to blacken
  7. Alternatively, bake chicken at 350 degrees for 25 minutes
  8. Spritz lemon juice onto chicken before serving
